Fruit Classification AI

This application utilizes a deep learning model to classify images of various fruits. The model is built upon the efficient MobileNetV2 architecture, trained on a diverse dataset to accurately identify fruit types from your uploads.

Currently, the model supports the following classes: Strawberry, apple, banana, cantaloupe, cherry, grape, kiwifruit, mango, orange, pineapple, and watermelon.
